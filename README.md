## 📌 What nexus does

nexus helps you test a business decision before you spend time or money on it.

It uses many AI agents to look at one idea from different sides. You can use it to check pricing, product plans, launch timing, hiring choices, and more. It gives you a clear simulation so you can see possible outcomes before you act.

## 🧠 How the simulation works

nexus creates a decision model and checks it with many AI agents. Each agent looks at the problem from a different angle.

You may see agents that act like:
- a risk checker
- a customer voice
- a finance reviewer
- a market analyst
- a product planner
- a growth planner
- a long-term strategy reviewer

This gives you a wider view than a single opinion. You can see where an idea may fail, where it may work, and what tradeoffs you need to weigh.

## 🛠️ How to use it well

To get better results, keep your input short and specific.

### Good input
- We want to raise our monthly price from $29 to $39.
- We have a small team and want to hire one engineer.
- We plan to launch in the US before Europe.

### Weak input
- Help me with my business.
- Tell me what to do.
- I need advice.

The more direct your question is, the better the simulation can work.

## 🔍 Reading the results

After a run, focus on these parts:

- likely upside
- main risk
- weak points in the plan
- tradeoffs
- next steps

Look for patterns across the agents. If many of them point to the same issue, that usually matters more than a single strong opinion.

## 🧭 Troubleshooting

### The file does not open
- Download it again
- Make sure the file finished downloading
- Check that you chose the Windows version

### Windows blocks the app
- Use the download from the GitHub release page
- Check the file name and version
- Run it again from your Downloads folder

### The app opens but shows no data
- Try a simpler input
- Add more details to your decision
- Check your internet connection
- Close and reopen the app

### The app feels slow
- Close other apps
- Use a smaller scenario first
- Try one decision at a time
